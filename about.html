About Me

I was an intern at Inkling working on Habitat, a web-based collaborative authoring system and before that I was at LoblawDigital doing full-stack web development helping to bring groceries online. Here are some languages and frameworks I've used in production environments: Java, JSP, lots of Javascript (ES6), HTML/LESS/SASS, Spring and Backbone. I also have experience with Python and Scheme as well as dabbled in Android development. Check out my projects!
